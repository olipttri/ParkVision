How to do the Test!

It's very simple and quick!

#1 Draw a Pattern:

Take a blank piece of paper and draw either a wave line or a spiral.

#2 Upload the Image:

Take a clear picture of your drawing and upload it to the website.

#3 View the Results:

The system will process the image using AI and display the results.

*This detection is not 100% accurate.

ParkVision

ParkVision is an AI platform that uses image analysis of drawing tests to detect early Parkinson’s tremor patterns, aiding accurate diagnosis and improved patient outcomes.
