Over 10 million people worldwide live with Parkinson's Disease.

14.2 per 100,000 people are diagnosed with Parkinson's yearly in Indonesia.

Parkinson's is progressive and has no cure yet—managing symptoms is key!

How to do the Test!

It's very simple and quick!

  1. #1 Draw a Pattern:
    Take a blank piece of paper and draw either a wave line or a spiral.
  2. #2 Upload the Image:
    Take a clear picture of your drawing and upload it to the website.
  3. #3 View the Results:
    The system will process the image using AI and display the results.
