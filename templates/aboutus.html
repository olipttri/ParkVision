ABOUT US

ParkVision is a cutting-edge AI platform developed to detect early signs of Parkinson's Disease using drawing pattern analysis. By analyzing simple sketches like spirals and wave lines, ParkVision leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to identify tremor patterns that might indicate early stages of Parkinson's Disease.

Our mission is to empower individuals and healthcare professionals with accessible, user-friendly tools for early screening and better symptom management. At ParkVision, we believe that technology can bridge the gap in early detection, providing insights that lead to timely medical intervention and improved patient outcomes.
